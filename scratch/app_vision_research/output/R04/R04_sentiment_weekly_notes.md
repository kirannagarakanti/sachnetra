# R04 — Weekly Sentiment Notes (April – May 2026)

*Compiled as of May 27, 2026. This document tracks the weekly qualitative sentiment shift among Indian retail traders across target subreddits over the past 8 weeks, tracing reactions to regulatory changes, geopolitical events, and macroeconomic data.*

---

## 8-Week Sentiment Tracker

### Week 1 (April 1 – April 7, 2026)
* **Market Context:** Union Budget 2026 amendments become active on April 1. Revised Securities Transaction Tax (STT) hikes (futures to 0.05%, options to 0.15%) and the elimination of weekly expiries except for one index per exchange are implemented.
* **Sentiment Profile:** `angry_at_broker` | `regulatory_fear`
* **Retail Narrative & Language (Hinglish/Hindi):**
  * *"Sarkar ne F&O khatam kar diya. Expiry trading is no longer profitable with 0.15% STT."* — Emotional venting in Hinglish dominates `r/IndianStreetBets` with traders complaining that transaction costs now eat up 40–50% of scalping profits.
  * *Discussion Focus:* Technical threads analyze whether Nifty 50 will find immediate support at 24,000 as volumes decline.
* **SachNetra Relevancy:** High policy/regulatory news signal (`event_type: regulation`).

### Week 2 (April 8 – April 14, 2026)
* **Market Context:** Options volumes show a visible drop. Traders begin discussing shifts from high-frequency options scalping to equity cash swing trading.
* **Sentiment Profile:** `confused` | `bullish` (shifting to cash swing)
* **Retail Narrative & Language (English/Hinglish):**
  * *"Shifting 70% of my capital from options buying to swing cash. Broker charges are too high, and swing trading gives better peace of mind."* — Transition narrative on `r/StockMarketIndia`.
  * *Discussion Focus:* Swing setups in mid-cap indices and identifying liquid large-caps.
* **SachNetra Relevancy:** Shift in retail interest toward cash stock-picking.

### Week 3 (April 15 – April 21, 2026)
* **Market Context:** Expiry session on April 16 is hit by technical lags and login issues on discount broker platforms.
* **Sentiment Profile:** `angry_at_broker`
* **Retail Narrative & Language (Hinglish/Hindi):**
  * *"Zerodha freeze again on expiry! Loss ho gaya system lag ki wajah se. Who will refund?"* — Heavy venting on `r/IndianStreetBets` about broker infrastructure stability.
  * *Discussion Focus:* Comparing Zerodha vs. Groww vs. institutional brokers like ICICI Direct.
* **SachNetra Relevancy:** High real-time infrastructure event signal (`event_type: infrastructure_outage`).

### Week 4 (April 22 – April 28, 2026)
* **Market Context:** Middle East (Israel-Iran) geopolitical tensions flare up. Brent crude spikes past $110/bbl. USD/INR spot edges past 95.20.
* **Sentiment Profile:** `bearish` | `regulatory_fear`
* **Retail Narrative & Language (English):**
  * *"Geopolitical risk is rising. Strait of Hormuz disruption could push oil past $120. Better to hedge with IT/Pharma or sit on cash."* — Technical and macro hedging discussions on `r/IndiaInvestments`.
  * *Discussion Focus:* Fear of gap-downs at market open.
* **SachNetra Relevancy:** Geopolitical events tracking (`event_type: geopolitical_shock`).

### Week 5 (April 29 – May 5, 2026)
* **Market Context:** Brent crude touches $117/bbl. RBI MPC maintains repo rate at 5.25% with a neutral stance. Wholesale inflation (WPI) releases at a high 8.30% print, contrasting with CPI at 3.48%.
* **Sentiment Profile:** `confused`
* **Retail Narrative & Language (Hinglish/Hindi):**
  * *"Why is WPI 8.3% but CPI only 3.48%? Hindi macro explainers trending on r/StockMarketIndia: WPI price rise will eventually hit corporate margins."* — Analytical focus in Hinglish on inflation dynamics.
  * *Discussion Focus:* Squeeze on Oil Marketing Companies (BPCL, IOC, HPCL) margins.
* **SachNetra Relevancy:** Content opportunities in macro explainers (`event_type: macro_commentary`).

### Week 6 (May 6 – May 12, 2026)
* **Market Context:** Nifty IT sector crash deepens. TCS reports a net headcount reduction of 23,460, and Infosys releases cautious hiring updates amid generative AI budget shifts.
* **Sentiment Profile:** `bearish`
* **Retail Narrative & Language (English/Hinglish):**
  * *"Indian IT is facing its Kodak moment. AI coding agents will replace mass offshore developers. Don't touch TCS/Infosys."* — Negative outlook on technology stocks on `r/IndianStreetBets`.
  * *Discussion Focus:* Are IT giants dinosaurs or value buys at multi-year low valuations?
* **SachNetra Relevancy:** AI disruption narrative tracking (`sector: IT`).

### Week 7 (May 13 – May 19, 2026)
* **Market Context:** Cumulative FPI MTD equity outflows cross -₹25,000 crore. However, domestic flows (SIP at ₹31k cr) absorb the selling. Metal and defence PSU sectors (BEL, HAL) break out to new highs.
* **Sentiment Profile:** `bullish` (on PSUs) | `bearish` (on Index)
* **Retail Narrative & Language (Hinglish/Hindi):**
  * *"DII SIP is the shield protecting Nifty from FII dumping. Also, defense stocks HAL and BEL are going parabolic!"* — High excitement and FOMO around government capex plays on `r/StockMarketIndia`.
  * *Discussion Focus:* Tracking Grey Market Premium (GMP) for Rajnandini Fashion IPO.
* **SachNetra Relevancy:** Flow monitoring and PSU rotations (`sector: defence`).

### Week 8 (May 20 – May 27, 2026)
* **Market Context:** USD/INR spot trades near 95.83. Nifty 50 consolidates around 23,900. Expiry day on May 27 sees VIX plunge 7% to 14.98. FPI MTD outflows reach -₹34,469 crore; DII cash MTD net buys reach +₹63,445 crore.
* **Sentiment Profile:** `confused` | `bullish` (option sellers)
* **Retail Narrative & Language (Hinglish/Hindi):**
  * *"Expiry decay was extremely fast today. Option sellers made easy money with VIX dropping 7%. Nifty holding 23,900 support."* — Positional updates on `r/IndianStreetBets`.
  * *Discussion Focus:* Analyzing Merritronix SME IPO lot size of ₹2.98 Lakh.
* **SachNetra Relevancy:** Expiry level dynamics and SME IPO speculation.
