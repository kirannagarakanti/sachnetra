# R00 — SachNetra Product Archetype Map

**Date Baseline:** May 27, 2026  
**Context:** Synthesis of competitor categories and product positioning in the Indian fintech landscape.

---

## 1. Product Archetype Survey

### News Aggregator
*   **Who uses it:** Casual retail investors, general salariat, and active market participants seeking broad daily updates.
*   **Winner in India:** Moneycontrol and Dailyhunt.
*   **SachNetra Relationship:** `win`
*   **Analysis:** SachNetra wins here by offering a 100% ad-free, noise-free experience with advanced features like Jaccard-based story clustering (already live) and high-precision G1 entity tagging. This directly bypasses the clickbait, intrusive pop-up ads, and sensationalist headline spin that plague incumbents like Moneycontrol and ET Markets.

### Market Terminal Lite
*   **Who uses it:** Technical day traders, F&O speculators, and retail swing traders.
*   **Winner in India:** TradingView and Trendlyne.
*   **SachNetra Relationship:** `partial`
*   **Analysis:** SachNetra has no charts, price tick engines, or option chains by design, but overlaps partially on the "lite terminal" desk view by displaying macro-flow indicators (such as FPI/DII net ratios) and direct filing feeds.

### Broker App (Execution + Bundled News)
*   **Who uses it:** Every retail participant executing cash equity, Mutual Fund, or F&O trades in India.
*   **Winner in India:** Zerodha Kite, Groww, and Angel One.
*   **SachNetra Relationship:** `avoid`
*   **Analysis:** SachNetra will not build execution pipelines, option trading screens, or margin accounts. It completely avoids broker execution licensing, capital requirements, and the massive operational overhead of client onboarding.

### Alt-Data / Sentiment B2B
*   **Who uses it:** Systematic quant funds, mid-tier prop desks, and fintech developers.
*   **Winner in India:** Heckyl, Tijori Finance (B2B feed), and global providers like FactSet.
*   **SachNetra Relationship:** `win`
*   **Analysis:** SachNetra wins by offering a cost-effective developer API (e.g., research option of ₹5,000–10,000/month) containing clean, structured news sentiment, sector tags, and PDF filing triggers. This solves the high pricing cliff of institutional data feeds that shuts out mid-tier builders.

### Research PDF / WhatsApp Informal
*   **Who uses it:** Retail day traders, SME IPO speculators, and informal chat communities.
*   **Winner in India:** Broker research desk PDFs (e.g., Motilal Oswal, HDFC Securities) and informal Telegram/WhatsApp tip groups.
*   **SachNetra Relationship:** `avoid`
*   **Analysis:** SachNetra completely avoids distributing unstructured, unregulated stock tips or crowdsourced buy/sell recommendations. This shields the platform from SEBI enforcement actions against unregistered advisory.

## 2. Where SachNetra Wins Without Becoming a Broker

SachNetra wins by acting as an **independent, ad-free, high-precision research and data infrastructure layer**. 

In May 2026, discount brokers are moving up the value chain by bundling news and fundamentals directly inside their execution tabs (e.g., Zerodha's Pulse and Tijori integration, Groww's news tab), creating a "broker bundle" threat (R09). However, brokers face a structural conflict of interest: they optimize for transaction volume and F&O churn to generate brokerage fees, while their restricted UI space cannot show comprehensive macro flow metrics or raw exchange filings. 

SachNetra wins by serving serious fundamental investors and active swing traders who demand unmanipulated context, domestic flow buffers, and direct PDF exchange filings without the noise of execution screens or the bias of brokerage-driven engagement loops.
