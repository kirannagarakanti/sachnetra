## Regulatory Implications for SachNetra
1. **Pump-and-Dump Filtering:** Stock-specific alerts originating from Telegram must not be aggregated as genuine news signals unless corroborated by formal exchange disclosures (Bulk/Block deals or corporate filings).
2. **Entity Safeguards:** SME stocks mentioned heavily in speculative channels should be flagged with surveillance alerts in the backend database.
3. **No Advisory Association:** As SachNetra expands, the platform must completely avoid indexing, linking, or referring to unregistered advisory services or subscription-based "tips" channels to maintain SEBI compliance.
