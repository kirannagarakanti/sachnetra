## Detailed Profile Reviews

### 1. Nifty Option Scalpers
* **Focus:** Intraday derivative technical levels and options flow.
* **Tone:** Highly aggressive, focus on quick scalp profits, panic during sharp reversals.
* **Significance for SachNetra:** Reveals the daily "resistance" and "support" psychological lines that retail options buyers watch. Useful for identifying structural sentiment anchors during session peaks.

### 2. PSU Stock Hunters
* **Focus:** Momentum trading in Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) and cyclical sectors (Metal, Energy).
* **Tone:** Bullish, patriotic, high-conviction "holding for long term" narratives that are actually short-term swing positions.
* **Significance for SachNetra:** Excellent indicator of sector rotations. If a specific PSU (like HAL or NTPC) is mentioned heavily in this channel, it correlates with volume spikes on the exchange.

### 3. Desi Trader Voice
* **Focus:** General retail rants, broker outages, tax complaints, and market psychology.
* **Tone:** Humorous, cynical, frustrated (particularly with SEBI regulations and STT hikes).
* **Significance for SachNetra:** The gold standard for retail market mood. Captures complaints about broker outages (Zerodha/Groww) and margin changes immediately after they occur, usually 2-3 hours before English press covers the disruption.

### 4. Market Pulse India
* **Focus:** Aggregating mainstream financial news headlines and exchange disclosures.
* **Tone:** Professional, objective, low-context.
* **Significance for SachNetra:** Extremely low incremental value. Almost all postings here are copy-pasted directly from ET, Mint, or NDTV Profit feeds. Useful only to confirm what mainstream news is reaching the Telegram ecosystem first.

### 5. IPO Alerts India
* **Focus:** SME and Mainboard IPO status tracking.
* **Tone:** Speculative, allotment-obsessed, GMP-focused.
* **Significance for SachNetra:** Collects Grey Market Premium (GMP) data, which is highly sought after by retail investors but completely absent from exchange filings. Provides a direct proxy of retail speculative liquidity.
