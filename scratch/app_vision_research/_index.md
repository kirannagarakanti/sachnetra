## How to use this pack

1. Open the brief `.md` in Antigravity; run searches per §Parts.
2. Save deliverables under `scratch/app_vision_research/output/<brief_id>/`.
3. Cite: URL, date, quote, confidence (`confirmed` | `anecdote` | `marketing`), **`lang:`** on social posts.
4. Tick checkbox below + one line in `research_log.md`.
5. Claude updates `app_vision_2026.md` per brief (**R03–R00 merged**).

**Hard rules:**

- Facts vs opinions separated.
- Public Reddit/Telegram only.
- No Exp 11 coupling.
