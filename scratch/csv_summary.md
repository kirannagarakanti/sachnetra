## Part E — API Mechanics, Revisions, Cadence & Licensing (Verified)

### 1. API Host Stability & Canonicality
* **API Host:** `stats.bis.org/api/v1` is the current, active, canonical host for programmatic SDMX REST API queries.
* **Relation to `data.bis.org`:** `data.bis.org` is a human-facing web visualization portal and search dashboard. It does not replace the REST API host. Programmatic systems must target `https://stats.bis.org/api/v1/data/<DATASET>/<KEY>?format=csv`.
* **Stability:** The host has been stable, requiring no authentication or cookie warm-up. Only a standard browser `User-Agent` is required.

### 2. BIS Terms of Use & Redistribution Citation
* **Attribution Requirement:** Any redistribution of data must attribute the source to the Bank for International Settlements.
* **Redistribution Limits:** Non-commercial redistribution of "limited extracts" (up to 400 words, or up to two tables/graphs, not exceeding 10% of the publication) is allowed free of charge. Larger redistributions or commercial usages require written permission from the BIS.
* **Canonical Attribution String:**
  > "Source: Bank for International Settlements (BIS); www.bis.org."
  *(This attribution string must be stored or displayed alongside the ingested records.)*

### 3. Revisions, Cadence, and Typical Lag per Dataset

The following table summarizes the revision behaviors, cadences, and lags for each of the 11 datasets verified for India:

| Dataset Code | Series Description | Cadence | Typical Release Lag | Revisions on Republish? |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `WS_CBPOL` | Central bank policy rates | Monthly / Daily | 0 days (immediate) | **No** (policy rates are definitive historical facts) |
| `WS_CBTA` | Central bank total assets | Monthly / Quarterly / Annual | 1 - 2 months | **Rarely** (final audited balance sheets are stable) |
| `WS_CREDIT_GAP` | Credit-to-GDP gaps | Quarterly | ~3 - 4 months | **Yes** (re-estimated HP trend and revised credit/GDP history modify all historical gap values) |
| `WS_DPP` | Detailed residential property prices | Quarterly | ~3 - 4 months | **Yes** (national statistics revisions affect historical index values) |
| `WS_DSR` | Debt service ratios | Quarterly | ~3 - 4 months | **Yes** (revised aggregate income, debt, and interest rates modify historical ratios) |
| `WS_EER` | Effective exchange rates | Monthly / Daily | ~15 days (monthly) / 0 days (daily) | **Yes** (monthly EER is recalculated historically when trade partner inflation/CPI is updated) |
| `WS_GLI` | Global liquidity indicators | Quarterly | ~3 - 4 months | **Yes** (reporting banks submit retrospective data revisions) |
| `WS_LONG_CPI` | Consumer prices statistics | Monthly / Annual | 1 - 2 months | **Rarely** (standard CPI is generally final once released, except during rebasings) |
| `WS_SPP` | Selected residential property prices | Quarterly | ~3 - 4 months | **Yes** (historical prices are frequently revised retrospectively) |
| `WS_TC` | Total credit to non-financial sectors | Quarterly | ~3 - 4 months | **Yes** (historical credit is revised retrospectively by central banks) |
| `WS_XRU` | US dollar exchange rates | Monthly | 0 - 1 day | **No** (bilateral historical spot rates are fixed) |
