# Portfolio Optimization

**TL;DR:** The mathematical process of allocating capital across multiple assets to maximize expected returns for a given level of risk or minimize risk for a target return.

## Dynamic Constraints
- **Transaction Costs:** Traditional optimization assumes zero or linear trading costs, causing excessive rebalancing. Integrating non-linear market impact models prevents this.
- **Cross-Impact Control:** Multi-asset portfolios must co-optimize trade scheduling for correlated assets to avoid combined cross-impact slippage.
